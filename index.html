Who is TonyTechnic?

I am many things depending on the individual you ask! Some people find my attitude and interest to be fondly endearing, while others may find it obnoxious. People often describe me as something of a renaissance man. I am an experienced writer, english tutor, photoshop designer, premiere + after effects editor, audacity audio editor, amateur web designer, avid gardener and fisherman.

In my free time I enjoy tinkering with special effects, watching anime, gaming and learning new vocabulary words. I'm an autistic person with main hyperfixations, and someone that always needs to be working on a project of some kind. Whether that be creating a YouTube poop, writing another short story or simply cultivating vegetables! I'm always willing to help my friends in any way I can, especially if it involves grammar or Adobe products.

What can TonyTechnic do?

My aspiration for writing and literature is my greatest trait. I have been writing short stories since I was a young child, and I intend to do so until the day that I die. Incapable of drawing due to my hand eye cordination, writing has always been my method to communicate directly from the heart. I write a variety of different subjects including urban fantasy, self-help / guidance, science fiction, inspirational anecdotes, autobiographies and so much more!

Outside of literature, I have a passion for video creation. Growing up on the classical era of the web, the dream of becoming a big YouTuber never dissipated, but lately I've focused more on learning new editing techniques than anything... I am moderately experienced in Photoshop, Audacity, After Effects and Premiere. I believe anyone with a healthy dream ought to act upon it, and I'm no different. If you want to make videos, just try making them, as you only get better with experience. Everyone's first video sucks ass and you need to accept that. We all do.
